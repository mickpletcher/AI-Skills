# Sample Idea

## Raw Concept

A modular cable routing clip for workshop benches that can be repositioned without tools.

## Basic Context

- It attaches to the edge or surface of a bench without permanent fasteners.
- It routes extension cords, power tool leads, and air hose trigger lines.
- It can be moved as the bench layout changes.
- The intended benefit is cleaner routing, less snagging, and faster workstation reconfiguration.

## Questions To Test The Skill

- What specific problem does this solve over common stick on clips or zip ties?
- What is mechanically novel about the repositioning mechanism?
- What components should be shown in drawings?
- What broad and fallback claim themes could fit?
