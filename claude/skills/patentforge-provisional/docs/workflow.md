# Workflow

## Purpose

This workflow turns an unstructured invention idea into a draft package that is easier to review, search, refine, and hand to counsel.

## End To End Flow

1. Capture the raw idea in the inventor's words.
2. Ask targeted disclosure questions only where the record is incomplete.
3. Convert the answers into a structured invention disclosure.
4. Identify the core technical components and how they interact.
5. Identify potential novelty points and combinations.
6. Build a prior art search strategy with keyword groups and possible CPC areas.
7. Draft claim candidates in broad and fallback form.
8. Draft a provisional style application package.
9. Produce a figure list for drawings.
10. Produce a manual Patent Center filing checklist.
11. Produce attorney review notes.
12. Add the required disclaimer.

## Intake Rules

- Preserve the inventor's meaning even when their wording is rough.
- Prefer follow up questions over silent assumptions when a missing fact affects novelty or claim framing.
- Keep confidential items visible in the disclosure but label them clearly if they should be handled carefully.

## Prior Art Rules

- Focus first on structure, function, problem, and result.
- Use synonyms and alternative terminology.
- Suggest CPC areas only as search starting points.
- Treat the search as directional, not exhaustive.

## Claim Drafting Rules

- Start from the broadest defensible technical concept in the inventor's disclosure.
- Separate essential features from optional implementation detail.
- Draft system and method themes when both make sense.
- Add fallback positions for narrower protection themes.
- Mark all claim candidates as drafting aids only.

## Provisional Draft Rules

- Keep the narrative technically complete enough to support later refinement.
- Include alternatives and variations wherever they may matter.
- Align the figure list to the written description.
- Keep filing support manual. Do not imply direct Patent Center automation.

## Output Rules

The final output should follow the section order defined in [`../SKILL.md`](../SKILL.md) and include the mandatory disclaimer every time.
