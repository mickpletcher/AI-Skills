# Patent Center Filing Checklist

Manual filing support only. Do not treat this as direct filing automation.

## Pre Filing Review

- Confirm inventor names and contact details
- Confirm title consistency across all draft materials
- Confirm the disclosure matches what the inventors actually built or conceived
- Confirm confidential business details are handled intentionally
- Confirm a registered patent attorney has reviewed the package if legal review is desired

## Draft Package Review

- Invention disclosure is complete
- Provisional draft sections are filled out
- Figure list is complete
- Claim candidates are marked as drafting aids only
- Prior art report notes are included with risk comments
- Attorney review notes identify open issues and missing detail

## Patent Center Preparation

- Confirm the filer has an active Patent Center account
- Confirm the correct filing entity and application type
- Prepare the final PDF or other accepted submission files
- Check that file names are clear and final
- Check that drawings are legible and correspond to the written description
- Confirm any required inventor declarations or supporting items outside this skill

## Manual Upload Steps

1. Sign in to Patent Center.
2. Start a new application using the correct application path for a provisional filing.
3. Enter bibliographic details carefully.
4. Upload the finalized documents manually.
5. Review the uploaded files and labels before submission.
6. Review fee information and filing details.
7. Submit only after the responsible filer confirms everything is correct.
8. Save the submission receipt and confirmation details.

## Final Reminder

- This skill does not submit to Patent Center
- The user is responsible for final Patent Center submission
